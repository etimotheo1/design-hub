Tanzania Market Leadership

Same-day delivery for Dar retailers

Stage:
Assignee:
Created by Sara · 2 May 2026
Category
Type
Deadline

Description

Currently retailers place orders 1–2 days in advance. Many small kiosks have no storage and want to order in the morning, get stock the same day.

What we imagine

Kiosk owners place an order on WhatsApp before 10am and receive stock by 4pm same day. Driver gets a route on his phone, customer gets an SMS when out for delivery.
